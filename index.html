Your Arrival

Monday, 6th of November, 1784. It's a rainy day. Mr. Jackson's driver waited in front of the railway station. Mr Jackson comes out of the railway station and the driver gets out of his car, opens the door and takes the suitcase into the trunk. He gets back into his car and said: "Hello, my name is Mr. Instance. Do you had a pleasant trip?" You tell him you had a pleasant trip and where you want to go now. He is surprised after you told him but he drove off the station. You arrive at the shire. It's very cold and foggy outside and you hurry towards the majestic door. It's rusty and difficult to open but you lean against it and at least it opens. The frontyard is huge and you need a few hundred steps to reach the big wooden door of the estate which is, to your surprise, open. You step in. There's no light in the entrance hall and your eyes first have to get accustomed to the dark, but this doesn't take a lot of time. Ther's another open door..
